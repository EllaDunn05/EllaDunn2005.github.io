Watermelon Mint Popsicles

Ingredients: 3 cups fresh watermelon, diced 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice 2 tablespoons honey or agave syrup (adjust to taste) 1-2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, chopped Instructions: Prepare the Watermelon: Remove seeds from the watermelon and cut it into small chunks. Blend the Ingredients: In a blender, combine the diced watermelon, lime juice, honey (or agave syrup), and chopped mint leaves. Blend until smooth and well combined. Strain (Optional): If you prefer a smoother texture, you can strain the mixture using a fine-mesh sieve to remove any pulp. Pour into Popsicle Molds: Pour the blended mixture into popsicle molds, leaving a little space at the top for expansion. Insert Sticks: Place the popsicle sticks into the molds. If your molds come with a lid, cover them to keep the sticks in place. If not, you can use aluminum foil and make small slits for the sticks. Freeze: Place the popsicle molds in the freezer and let them freeze for at least 4-6 hours or until completely solid. Unmold and Enjoy: Once the popsicles are frozen, run the molds briefly under warm water to loosen the popsicles. Gently remove the popsicles from the molds and enjoy these cool, minty watermelon delights! These Watermelon Mint Popsicles are a burst of summer sweetness with a hint of refreshing mint. They're perfect for kids and adults alike, making them an ideal treat for hot summer days. Feel free to get creative and experiment with different herbs or add a sprinkle of Tajin or chili powder for a unique twist!
